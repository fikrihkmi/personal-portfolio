This section includes the biodata of Fikri Hakimi

Hakimi's full name is Muhamad Fikri Hakimi bin Mat Yunus. He was born on 30th September 2000 at a hospital in Kota Bharu kelantan. He comes from a normal family and currently lives in Kampung Tembesu Limbat, Kota Bharu, Kelantan. In his past time, he loves to read fictional books and playing various type of sports.


In addition, Hakimi is a graduate from UiTM Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Machang with Bachelor of Information Science (Information Management Systems) (Hons).
